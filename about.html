About Our Institution

Established with a profound vision to bridge the gap between heavy industrial requirements and trained technical human resources, Government ITI has stood as a pioneer hub for technical training programs over decades.

Our Vision

To cultivate a globally recognized training ecosystem that transforms raw student caliber into premium operational standards across diverse industrial formats.

Our Mission

  • Provide experiential learning using next-generation tooling environments.
  • Up-skill students on dynamic digital technologies including contemporary programming languages and automation logic.
  • Empower local socio-economic groups via accessible, low-cost practical education frameworks.
